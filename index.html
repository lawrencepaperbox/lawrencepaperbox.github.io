What We do

Lawrence Paper Box Company specializes in providing paperboard die-cutting services and in supplying grey board and mill board materials. Our company has served clients whose manufacturing needs range from jewelery boxes to stationary files and folders to caps and shirt supports. We specialize in catering to customized manufacturing needs!


Our services include flat die-cutting, shearing and punching. We cater to custom designs as well. We also supply raw materials such as grey-board, paper board and mill board.


Why Us?

Lawrence Paper Box Company has made a name for itself in the community through our reliable service, great work ethic and use of environmentally friendly recycled materials. Order with us and you will definitely not be disappointed!
